How to Negotiate Watch Prices

Updated January 2026

← Back to Home

Can you negotiate? It depends where you're buying.

Authorized Dealers

  • 5-15% discount often possible on available pieces
  • Ask politely: "Is there flexibility on price?"
  • End of quarter/year offers better chances
  • Cash payment may help

Grey Market

  • Pricing already discounted 20-40%
  • Ask if price is negotiable—expected
  • Wire payment may yield 2-3% discount

Pre-Owned

  • Most negotiable category
  • Know market prices first
  • Point out flaws to justify lower offers
  • Be prepared to walk away

What NOT to Do

  • Don't be rude or aggressive
  • Don't waste time with no purchase intent
  • Don't burn bridges—watch world is small
