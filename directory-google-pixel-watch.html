About Google Pixel Watch

After years of powering other manufacturers' smart watches with Wear OS, Google launched its own Pixel Watch in October 2022. The watch represented Google's vision of what a smart watch should be: elegant, intelligent, and deeply integrated with Google services.

Pixel Watch combines Wear OS 4 with Fitbit's health and fitness expertise (following Google's 2021 acquisition). Users get the best of both worlds: Google Assistant, Google Maps, Google Wallet, and Google Home controls alongside Fitbit's industry-leading sleep tracking and Active Zone Minutes.

The distinctive domed design, recycled aluminum case, and customizable bands make Pixel Watch as much a fashion statement as a tech device. With each generation, Google has improved battery life, display brightness, and health sensors.
