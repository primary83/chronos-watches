Understanding Wrist Size & Watch Fit

For wrists under 6.5 inches (16.5cm), these guidelines help ensure proportional fit:

  • Case diameter: 34-40mm ideal (36-38mm sweet spot)
  • Lug-to-lug: Under 48mm critical (under 46mm ideal)
  • Thickness: Under 12mm preferred
  • Lug shape: Curved or short lugs fit better

üí° The Lug-to-Lug Rule

Lug-to-lug measurement matters more than case diameter. A 40mm watch with long lugs (50mm L2L) will wear larger than a 42mm watch with short lugs (47mm L2L). Always check lug-to-lug before purchasing‚Äîthe lugs shouldn't overhang your wrist.
