Watch Sizes Guide

8 min readUpdated January 2025

Understanding watch sizes is essential for finding the perfect fit. Here's everything you need to know.

Key Measurements

  • Case Diameter — Width of the watch face (most common measurement)
  • Lug-to-Lug — Total vertical length including lugs
  • Case Thickness — How tall the watch sits on your wrist
  • Lug Width — Determines strap compatibility

Size Recommendations by Wrist

Wrist CircumferenceRecommended Size
Under 6" (15cm)34-38mm
6" - 7" (15-18cm)38-42mm
7" - 7.5" (18-19cm)40-44mm
Over 7.5" (19cm+)42-46mm+

The Golden Rule

The watch lugs should never extend past the edges of your wrist. If they overhang, the watch is too big.
