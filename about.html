About Authentic Wrist

Independent editorial guide to luxury timepieces

What We Are (And What We're Not)

Authentic Wrist is an independent editorial platform. We research, review, and recommend watches — then connect you with trusted authorized dealers and vetted secondary-market retailers. We never sell watches directly. We have no inventory, no showroom, no sales targets. Our only agenda is helping you find the right timepiece.

How We Work

1

We Research

Our team studies watches across every price point — specifications, history, market positioning, real-world ownership experiences. We read forums, talk to collectors, and handle pieces whenever possible.

2

We Write Honest Guides

No brand pays for coverage or placement. We recommend watches we'd buy ourselves. When a watch has flaws, we say so. When a cheaper option outperforms an expensive one, we tell you.

3

We Connect You to Trusted Sellers

When you're ready to buy, we link to authorized dealers and established secondary-market retailers. We vet these partners for reputation and customer service. You buy from them, not us.

4

We Earn Through Affiliate Commissions

When you purchase through our links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund our work — but it never influences what we recommend. We suggest watches we believe in, regardless of affiliate relationships.

Our Editorial Values

Independence

No brand sponsors our content. Recommendations are based on merit, not partnerships.

Transparency

We clearly disclose affiliate relationships. You always know how we make money.

Accessibility

We cover $50 Casios with the same care as $50,000 Pateks. Great watches exist at every price.

Honesty

We acknowledge limitations, call out overpriced pieces, and admit when we don't know something.

Why Trust Us?

We're watch enthusiasts who got tired of biased reviews, hidden agendas, and content that seemed written for search engines rather than people. We built Authentic Wrist as the resource we wished existed — clear, honest, comprehensive.

Our team includes collectors, former retail professionals, and horological researchers. We don't claim to know everything, but we commit to being straightforward about what we do know and transparent about our limitations.

We have no inventory to move, no brand partnerships to protect, no advertising pressure. When we recommend a watch, it's because we genuinely think it's worth your consideration — not because someone paid us to say so.
