Why a Watch for Graduation?

  • Lasting significance: A watch can last a lifetime with proper care
  • Daily reminder: Worn constantly, it connects graduate to milestone
  • Professional asset: A quality watch signals maturity in the workplace
  • Heirloom potential: Can be passed to future generations
  • Personalization: Many watches can be engraved on the caseback

üí° The Engraving Touch

A caseback engraving transforms a nice watch into a treasured keepsake. Consider: graduation date, degree, a short message, or school name. Most jewelers can engrave watches with solid casebacks for $20-50. Check if the watch has an exhibition caseback (glass) before planning to engrave.
