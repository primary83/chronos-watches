Which Garmin Should You Buy?

Forerunner 165: Best entry-level for runners. Core metrics, AMOLED display, great price.

Forerunner 265/965: For dedicated runners wanting advanced training metrics. 265 for most; 965 for elites wanting maps and titanium.

Venu 3: For those wanting fitness tracking in a more lifestyle-focused smartwatch. Best for casual athletes and gym-goers.

Instinct 2: For outdoor adventurers on a budget. Rugged, long battery, great value. Solar version for extended trips.

Fenix 8: For multisport athletes wanting premium features. Dive mode, maps, every sensor imaginable.

Epix Pro: Like Fenix but with AMOLED display. Premium multisport with best-in-class screen.

Note: All Garmin watches are compatible with both iPhone and Android. Garmin Connect app required for full features.
