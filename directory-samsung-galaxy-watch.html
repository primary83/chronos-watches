Which Galaxy Watch Should You Buy?

Galaxy Watch FE: Best budget option. All essential health and fitness features at the lowest price. Great first smartwatch.

Galaxy Watch 7: Best for most people. Latest processor, AI features, comprehensive health tracking. The sweet spot for price/performance.

Galaxy Watch 6 Classic: For those who love the rotating bezel and want a more traditional watch look. Premium stainless steel construction.

Galaxy Watch Ultra: For serious athletes and outdoor adventurers. Titanium durability, 100-hour battery, extreme water resistance. Overkill for casual users.

Note: Galaxy Watch works best with Samsung Galaxy phones, but is compatible with most Android phones running Android 11+. Limited functionality with iPhone.
