About Samsung Galaxy Watch

Samsung entered the smartwatch market in 2013 with the Galaxy Gear, but it was the Galaxy Watch series launched in 2018 that truly established Samsung as a smartwatch leader. The brand is known for its circular designs, rotating bezels, and vibrant Super AMOLED displays.

In 2021, Samsung partnered with Google to create Wear OS 3, bringing Google's app ecosystem to Samsung's hardware excellence. The Galaxy Watch now offers the best of both worlds: Samsung's health sensors (including FDA-cleared ECG and blood pressure monitoring) with Google's vast app library.

The 2024 Galaxy Watch Ultra marks Samsung's entry into the rugged adventure watch segment, competing directly with Apple Watch Ultra and Garmin's high-end offerings.
