What Military Personnel Need

  • Reliability: Works every time, any condition
  • Durability: Survives abuse, impacts, elements
  • Legibility: Readable instantly in any light
  • Low profile: No snagging, fits under cuffs
  • Quiet operation: No audible ticking
  • No reflections: Matte finishes, subdued colors

Deployment Ready: Under $200

Casio G-Shock DW5600

$50 - $70

The unofficial military standard. Indestructible, water resistant to 200m, no reflective surfaces. Thousands deployed wearing these. Affordable enough to replace if lost.

Timex Expedition Gallatin

$50 - $65

Solar powered, rugged construction, Indiglo backlight. The fabric strap won't reflect. Simple, reliable, purpose-built for field use.

Marathon Navigator

$150 - $200

Actual military contract watch. Tritium tubes for constant glow without charging. Swiss quartz, fiberglass composite case. This is what gets issued.

Veterans: Civilian Life
