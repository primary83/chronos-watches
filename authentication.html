How Brands Ensure Authenticity

Serial Numbers

Every luxury watch has a unique serial number engraved on the case. Authorized dealers register this number with the manufacturer at the time of sale, creating a permanent record of ownership.

Security Features

Modern luxury watches include numerous anti-counterfeiting measures:

  • Micro-engravings ‚Äî Tiny details impossible to replicate accurately
  • Laser-etched logos ‚Äî Often visible only under magnification
  • Holographic elements ‚Äî On warranty cards and documentation
  • Unique movements ‚Äî In-house calibers with proprietary components
  • Material quality ‚Äî Specific alloys and finishing only available to the brand
