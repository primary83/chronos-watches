Every current Tudor model. Black Bay, Pelagos, Ranger - Rolex quality at accessible prices. Born to dare.

About Tudor

Founded in 1926 by Hans Wilsdorf (Rolex's founder) to offer quality watches at more accessible prices. Today Tudor makes its own in-house movements and has become a collector favorite. Known for the Black Bay line and excellent value proposition. Read full Tudor history →
