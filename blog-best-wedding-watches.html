Your wedding watch will appear in photos forever and remind you of that day every time you wear it. This guide covers elegant timepieces that complement formal wear and mark life's most important moment.

What Makes a Wedding Watch

  • Thin profile: Slides under French cuffs elegantly
  • Dress aesthetics: Clean dial, minimal complications
  • Appropriate size: 36-40mm for formal occasions
  • Quality leather: Or dressy bracelet (no dive watches)

Under $1,000

Orient Bambino

$150 - $200

Automatic dress watch at remarkable value. Domed crystal, in-house movement. Perfect if the wedding budget is focused elsewhere.

Tissot Le Locle

$395 - $550

Swiss heritage, classic styling. The Powermatic 80 version offers 80-hour reserve. Refined enough for any wedding.

$1,000 - $5,000

Nomos Tangente

$1,780 - $2,400

Bauhaus elegance, German manufacture. In-house movement, minimal design. The thinking groom's choice.

Cartier Tank Must

$2,920

Icon of 20th century design. Rectangle case, Roman numerals. Photos will never date.

Omega De Ville Prestige

$3,500 - $4,500

Co-Axial movement, classic proportions. Thin enough for formal wear, substantial enough to feel meaningful.

$5,000+

Rolex Datejust 36

$7,650+

The classic celebration watch. Multiple dial/bezel configurations. Start marriage with the definitive dress watch.

Jaeger-LeCoultre Reverso

$6,800+

Art Deco icon with flip case. Engrave a message on the caseback for wedding day. Unique design photographs beautifully.
