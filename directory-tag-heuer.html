Every current TAG Heuer model. Motorsport heritage meets Swiss precision since 1860.

About TAG Heuer

Founded in 1860 in Saint-Imier, Switzerland. Pioneered sports timing - invented the oscillating pinion (1887) and first dashboard chronograph (1911). Deep ties to motorsport through partnerships with McLaren, Porsche, Formula 1. Part of LVMH. Known for Carrera, Monaco, and accessible luxury. Read full TAG Heuer history →
