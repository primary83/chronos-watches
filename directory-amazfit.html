About Amazfit

Amazfit is a brand of Zepp Health (formerly Huami), founded in 2015. The company was Xiaomi's exclusive partner for wearables, helping produce the wildly popular Mi Band series before launching its own brand of feature-rich smart watches.

Amazfit's mission is to democratize advanced health technology. Their watches offer features typically found in devices costing 2-3x more: AMOLED displays, multi-day battery life, dual-band GPS, SpO2 monitoring, and comprehensive fitness tracking.

The brand has shipped over 200 million wearable devices globally and is known for industry-leading battery life—the GTR 4 offers 14 days of typical use compared to 1-2 days for competitors. Their Zepp OS powers AI features like the Zepp Coach for personalized training plans.
