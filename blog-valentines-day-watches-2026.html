Shopping Tips for Valentine's Watches

Know Their Wrist Size: If you're surprising them, try to measure an existing watch or bracelet. Most women's watches are 28-36mm; most men's are 38-44mm. When in doubt, err toward the middle of these ranges.

Consider Their Style: Do they dress formally or casually? Do they prefer gold, silver, or rose gold jewelry? Do they like minimalist design or more detailed pieces? Match the watch to their existing aesthetic.

Think About Their Lifestyle: An active partner might prefer something with water resistance and durability. A desk-job professional might appreciate a dressier piece. Consider how the watch will actually be worn.

Check Return Policies: Especially for surprise gifts, ensure you can exchange for a different size or style if needed. Most authorized retailers offer generous Valentine's return windows.

Don't Forget the Box: Presentation matters. Most watches come in beautiful packaging that adds to the gifting experience. Some brands offer special Valentine's packaging or gift wrapping‚Äîask when purchasing.
