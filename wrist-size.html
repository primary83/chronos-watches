How to Measure Your Wrist

Wrap a flexible tape measure (or a strip of paper) around your wrist just above the wrist bone. Keep it snug but not tight. If using paper, mark where it overlaps and measure the length with a ruler. Most men's wrists are 6.5" – 7.5" (16.5 – 19cm). Most women's wrists are 5.5" – 6.5" (14 – 16.5cm).
