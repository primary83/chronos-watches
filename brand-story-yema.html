The History of Yema

January 2025 · 9 min read

Yema offers something rare: genuine French watchmaking heritage including space exploration history. Founded in 1948, the brand equipped French astronauts and divers before declining and being revived as a credible heritage brand.

Besançon Origins

Henry Louis Belmont founded Yema in Besançon, France, in 1948. Besançon was France's watchmaking capital; Yema would become one of its most successful brands, producing quality watches that competed with Swiss imports on French wrists.

Tool Watch Development

Yema developed serious tool watches through the 1960s-70s. The Superman dive watch offered genuine underwater capability. The Rallygraf served motorsport timing. These weren't fashion pieces but functional instruments.

Spationaute: Space Heritage

In 1982, Jean-Loup Chrétien became the first Western European in space, wearing a Yema Spationaute. This genuine space heritage—not marketing arrangement but actual equipment selection—gives Yema credibility few brands can claim.

Decline

The quartz crisis and changing ownership took their toll. Yema passed through various hands, quality declined, and the brand lost relevance. Like many heritage names, it risked becoming mere trademark exploitation.

Revival

New ownership in the 2010s began proper revival. Rather than cheap licensing, they invested in manufacturing, developed in-house capabilities, and created watches honoring heritage while meeting contemporary expectations.

In-House Movement

Yema developed the YEMA2000 in-house movement—significant achievement for brand at this price point. French manufacture of both watches and movements distinguishes Yema from competitors assembling Asian components.

Superman Heritage

Modern Superman dive watches honor 1960s heritage with contemporary specifications. These aren't mere reissues but thoughtful updates serving actual divers while maintaining visual connection to celebrated predecessors.

Yema Today

For collectors wanting French watchmaking heritage—genuine history, domestic manufacture, accessible pricing—Yema offers authentic alternative to Swiss dominance. The space connection alone distinguishes them from most competitors.
