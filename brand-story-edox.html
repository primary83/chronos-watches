The History of Edox

January 2025 · 9 min read

Edox—Greek for "hour"—has specialized in water-resistant sports watches since the 1960s. With innovations including the first twin-crown dive watch system and partnerships with powerboat racing, Edox offers serious tool watches at accessible Swiss prices.

Swiss Foundations

Christian Ruefli-Flury founded Edox in 1884 in Biel/Bienne, Switzerland's watchmaking heartland. For decades, Edox produced quality Swiss watches without particular distinction. The transformation came in the 1960s when the brand focused on water resistance innovation.

Delfin: Double O-Ring

In 1961, Edox introduced the Delfin with a revolutionary double O-ring crown system. This innovation significantly improved water resistance without requiring screw-down crowns. The Delfin name—dolphin—signaled aquatic ambition that defined Edox's future.

Hydro-Sub

The Hydro-Sub dive watch line demonstrated Edox's commitment to serious underwater tools. Professional specifications, robust construction, and readable designs attracted divers seeking Swiss quality without luxury brand premiums.

Class-1 Racing Partnership

Edox became official timing partner for Class-1 World Powerboat Championship—the Formula 1 of offshore racing. This partnership wasn't random: powerboat racing demands water resistance under extreme conditions. Edox watches proved themselves in the harshest marine environments.

Chronoffshore-1

The Chronoffshore-1 collection, developed for powerboat racing, features aggressive styling, chronograph complications, and extreme water resistance. These watches target motorsport enthusiasts wanting watches reflecting their passion.

Les Bémonts Classic Line

Not all Edox watches scream sport. The Les Bémonts line offers classical dress watches, demonstrating range beyond dive and racing watches. Swiss quality and traditional aesthetics serve those preferring understatement.

Value Position

Edox occupies mid-market Swiss territory: genuine Swiss manufacture with quality movements at prices below luxury brands. For buyers wanting Swiss credibility without five-figure prices, Edox delivers substantial value.

Edox Today

Edox continues developing sports-focused collections while maintaining classical options. The brand offers Swiss watchmaking for enthusiasts seeking quality and water resistance without the premiums attached to more famous names.
