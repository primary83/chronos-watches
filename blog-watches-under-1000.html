Buying Guide

Best Watches Under $1,000

January 2, 2025 · 10 min read

You don't need to spend thousands to own a quality timepiece. The sub-$1,000 market has never been stronger, with respected brands delivering automatic movements, sapphire crystals, and serious craftsmanship at accessible prices.

This price range is perfect for first-time collectors, daily beaters you won't stress about, or adding variety to an existing collection without significant investment.

Top Picks Under $1,000

Best Overall

Tissot

PRX Powermatic 80 Editor's Choice

$675
40mm · Powermatic 80 automatic · 100m WR · sapphire crystal

The PRX revived the integrated bracelet sports watch at an accessible price. With 80-hour power reserve, Swiss automatic movement, and design DNA echoing watches costing 10x more, it's arguably the best value in watches today.

Best Dive Watch

Orient

Kamasu

$275
41.8mm · In-house automatic · 200m WR · sapphire crystal

Orient's in-house movement, sapphire crystal, and solid construction at this price is remarkable. The Kamasu offers true dive watch specs with sunburst dials that punch way above their weight class.

Best Dress Watch

Orient

Bambino Version 2

$165
40.5mm · Automatic · 30m WR · domed mineral crystal

Dress watch elegance with an automatic movement at a price that seems like an error. The domed crystal creates a vintage aesthetic that pairs perfectly with suits. The gateway drug to watch collecting.

Shop on Amazon →

Best Field Watch

Hamilton

Khaki Field Mechanical

$495
38mm · H-50 hand-wound · 50m WR · sapphire crystal

Hamilton supplied watches to the U.S. military, and that heritage lives here. The hand-wound H-50 offers 80-hour power reserve in an historically accurate 38mm case. Rugged elegance defined.

Shop on Amazon →

Best Chronograph

Seiko

Prospex Speedtimer Solar

$525
39mm · Solar chronograph · 100m WR · sapphire crystal

Solar-powered chronograph with panda dial aesthetic clearly inspired by a certain famous moonwatch. Never needs batteries, and the 39mm case is perfect for those finding modern chronographs too large.

Shop on Amazon →

The Bottom Line

The sub-$1,000 category has never offered more value. Brands like Seiko, Orient, Tissot, and Hamilton deliver genuine horological quality that would have been unthinkable at these prices twenty years ago.

These aren't compromises—they're legitimate timepieces that many collectors continue wearing alongside far more expensive pieces. Start here, and you'll understand what the watch hobby is all about.
