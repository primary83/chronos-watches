The History of Norqain

January 2025 · 9 min read

Norqain asks: can a new Swiss brand compete with established names on quality rather than heritage? Founded in 2018 by industry veterans, Norqain has rapidly gained respect for well-made sports watches that punch above their price class.

Industry Veterans

Ben Küffer, whose family has generations of Swiss watch industry experience (his father was CEO of both Breitling and Swatch Group brands), founded Norqain in Nidau, Switzerland. He assembled a team of industry professionals who understood what makes watches successful—and where established brands leave opportunities.

The Norqain Philosophy

Norqain targets the gap between fashion watches and luxury brands: well-made Swiss watches with genuine quality, independent ownership, and fair pricing. They emphasize Swiss manufacture, quality movements, and solid construction without heritage markup.

Adventure Collection

The Adventure collection offers robust sports watches designed for actual use. Durable construction, legible dials, and practical features serve outdoor enthusiasts wanting quality tools without the fragility of dress watches or the compromises of fashion brands.

Independence Collection

The Independence collection provides dressier options with the same quality standards. These watches serve professional environments where Adventure pieces might be too casual, maintaining Norqain's emphasis on build quality and Swiss manufacture.

Movement Partnership

Norqain partnered with Kenissi, the movement manufacturer part-owned by Tudor/Rolex, for exclusive calibers. This partnership provides access to high-quality movements typically reserved for brands with far larger production volumes.

NEVEREST Technology

Norqain developed NEVEREST shock protection technology, demonstrating commitment to proprietary innovation rather than simply assembling standard components. This kind of technical development distinguishes serious watchmakers from assemblers.

Rapid Growth

Since 2018, Norqain has expanded distribution, gained media attention, and built collector following. The brand appears in conversations alongside established names—remarkable achievement for such a young company.

Norqain Today

For collectors seeking Swiss quality without paying for heritage they don't value—who want their money spent on manufacturing rather than marketing history—Norqain offers compelling contemporary proposition.
