Women's Guide

Best Women's Dive Watches 2025

10 min read Updated January 2025

Women deserve dive watches that don't compromise on specifications just to reduce size. These timepieces offer professional-grade water resistance and functionality in sizes that fit smaller wrists beautifully.

What to Look For

  • Case size 34-40mm — Proportional to smaller wrists
  • Minimum 200m water resistance — True diving capability
  • Unidirectional bezel — Essential safety feature
  • Luminous markers — Visibility underwater
  • Screw-down crown — Proper water protection

Luxury Women's Divers

Rolex Submariner 36mm

$9,100

The newest Submariner comes in a 36mm case perfect for smaller wrists. Same professional specs as its larger sibling with 300m water resistance and Rolex reliability.

Case: 36mm Oystersteel
Movement: Rolex 3230
Power Reserve: 70 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Omega Seamaster Diver 300M 28mm

$5,100

Omega's ladies' Seamaster offers the same wave-pattern dial and helium escape valve as the men's model in a refined 28mm case. True dive watch functionality in an elegant package.

Case: 28mm Steel
Movement: Omega 8800
Power Reserve: 55 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Tudor Black Bay 32

$2,875

Tudor's Black Bay in 32mm offers the brand's signature dive watch aesthetics in a compact size. The smaller case maintains 150m water resistance.

Case: 32mm Steel
Movement: Tudor T201
Power Reserve: 38 hours
Water Resistance: 150m

Mid-Range Options

Oris Aquis 36.5mm

$2,200

Oris sized down their popular Aquis without compromising specs. Full 300m water resistance, ceramic bezel, and the brand's excellent finishing.

Case: 36.5mm Steel
Movement: Oris 733
Power Reserve: 38 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Longines HydroConquest 32mm

$1,175

Exceptional value in a ladies' diver. The HydroConquest offers Swiss quality, ceramic bezel, and 300m water resistance at an accessible price.

Case: 32mm Steel
Movement: L152
Power Reserve: 72 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Affordable Options

Seiko Prospex "Mini Turtle" SRPH57

$495

Seiko's compact take on their legendary Turtle case. At 38mm, it's more versatile while maintaining 200m water resistance and Seiko's reliable 4R35 movement.

Case: 38mm Steel
Movement: Seiko 4R35
Power Reserve: 41 hours
Water Resistance: 200m

Sizing Tip

Don't be afraid to try men's watches! Many women comfortably wear 38-40mm dive watches. Try before you buy — wrist shape matters more than size.

Our Recommendation

For serious divers, the Oris Aquis 36.5mm offers the best balance of specs and size. For luxury, the Rolex Submariner 36mm is the new benchmark.
