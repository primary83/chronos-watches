Magnetism and Watches

7 min read

Magnetism is the enemy of mechanical watches, causing them to run fast or erratically.

Common Sources

  • Smartphones and tablets
  • Laptop speakers
  • Magnetic clasps (bags, cases)
  • MRI machines
  • Security gates

Signs of Magnetization

  • Watch runs significantly fast (minutes per day)
  • Erratic timekeeping
  • Complete stoppage in severe cases

Solutions

  • Professional demagnetization (quick and inexpensive)
  • Home demagnetizers available
  • Consider anti-magnetic watches (Omega > 15,000 gauss)
