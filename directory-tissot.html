About Tissot

Founded in 1853 in Le Locle, Switzerland, Tissot has been delivering Swiss quality at accessible prices for over 170 years. Part of the Swatch Group, Tissot pioneered touchscreen watches and continues to innovate while maintaining traditional Swiss craftsmanship. Read full Tissot history →
