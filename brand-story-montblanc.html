The History of Montblanc Watches

January 2025 · 10 min read

From writing instruments to wristwatches. Montblanc—famous for its pens—has transformed into a serious watchmaker through the acquisition of historic Minerva.

The Pen Heritage

Montblanc was founded in 1906 as a pen manufacturer in Hamburg, Germany. The white star logo represents Mont Blanc, Europe's highest mountain.

Minerva: Instant Credibility

In 2007, Montblanc acquired Minerva—a manufacture founded in 1858 with genuine chronograph expertise. This wasn't just acquiring a supplier; it was acquiring 150 years of watchmaking heritage.

Key Collections

  • 1858: Vintage mountain exploration theme
  • Heritage: Classic dress watches
  • TimeWalker: Modern sports chronographs
  • Star Legacy: Elegant complications

For those who thought Montblanc would always be "just a pen company," the watches prove otherwise. Real manufacture. Real heritage. Real watchmaking.
