The History of Audemars Piguet

December 2024 · 11 min read

To break the rules, you must first master them. Since 1875, Audemars Piguet has crafted complicated watches in Le Brassus, creating the legendary Royal Oak.

Jules Louis Audemars and Edward Auguste Piguet founded their company in 1875. In 1972, Gerald Genta designed the Royal Oak—a luxury sports watch in steel that revolutionized the industry.

Key Collections

Royal Oak, Royal Oak Offshore, Code 11.59, Millenary

Still family-owned, AP remains fiercely independent and continues to push boundaries from its Vallee de Joux home.
