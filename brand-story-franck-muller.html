The History of Franck Muller

December 2024 · 10 min read

Master of Complications. Franck Muller creates extravagant timepieces with revolutionary complications in distinctive tonneau cases.

Franck Muller, a master watchmaker, founded the brand in 1991 after establishing himself as a restorer of vintage complications. His Crazy Hours and Revolution movements showcased unconventional thinking.

Key Collections

Vanguard, Crazy Hours, Long Island, Cintrée Curvex, Aeternitas Mega

Based in Genthod, Franck Muller continues to create some of the most complicated and visually striking watches in the world.
