Best Silver Anniversary Watches 2026 — 25 Years Together

April 2026 · 11 min read
Home / Guides / Best Silver Anniversary Watches 2026 — 25 Years Together
← Back to Guides

Twenty-five years of marriage is a staggering achievement — a quarter century of partnership, compromise, growth, and love. The silver anniversary watch should reflect the weight of that accomplishment with the elegance of silver: silver dials, polished steel cases, and the kind of quality that says "we're just getting started on the next twenty-five."

For Her

Omega Constellation 28mm (Silver Dial)
$3,600–$4,200

The Constellation's name means "group of stars" — appropriate for the woman who's been your North Star for 25 years. The silver dial with diamond hour markers on the women's 28mm catches light the way silver anniversary celebrations should: brilliantly, warmly, and with depth. The METAS-certified Co-Axial movement provides the precision that 25 years of partnership has taught you both to value.

Best for: Silver anniversary luxury for her.

Longines DolceVita with Diamond Indices
$1,200–$1,600

"La Dolce Vita" — the sweet life you've built together over 25 years. The rectangular case with diamond indices reflects the silver theme through brilliant light. Swiss Made, sapphire crystal, and the kind of quiet elegance that matches a woman who's been beautiful for a quarter century. Engrave "25 Silver Years" and it becomes the watch she wears to every anniversary dinner going forward.

Best for: Elegant silver anniversary at an accessible luxury price.

For Him

Omega Seamaster Aqua Terra (Silver Dial)
$5,500–$6,200

The silver-dial Aqua Terra matches the silver anniversary theme perfectly. The teak-textured dial catches light in shifting silver tones. The METAS Master Chronometer certification represents the precision and commitment that 25 years of marriage requires. On a steel bracelet, it's the silver anniversary watch that he wears daily as a reminder of what you've built together.

Best for: Silver anniversary luxury for him.

Tudor Black Bay 41 (Silver/Grey Dial)
$3,300–$3,575

The grey/silver dial Black Bay on steel bracelet is pure silver anniversary energy — the tones match, the quality reflects the milestone, and Tudor's "Born to Dare" ethos matches the courage it takes to build a 25-year marriage. In-house movement with 70-hour power reserve. Engrave the date and anniversary number on the caseback.

Best for: Silver anniversary quality at Tudor value.

Matching Pair

Tissot PRX 35mm (Her) + 40mm (Him) — Silver Dials
$590–$1,390 for the pair

Matching silver-dial PRX watches — same design, different proportions. The silver dials match the anniversary theme. The Swiss Made quality honors 25 years of choosing each other. Matching watches say "we're still a team" in a way that individual gifts don't. At $590-$1,390 for the pair, it's the most affordable matching silver anniversary gift from a quality Swiss brand.

Best for: Matching his-and-hers silver anniversary watches.

The Silver Anniversary Truth

You made it to 25 years. That's rarer and more valuable than any watch. The silver anniversary watch doesn't celebrate the object — it celebrates the time. Choose something in silver tones, engrave it with your years, and wear it as proof that some things really do get better with time. The next 25 starts now.
