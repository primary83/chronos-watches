The History of Hamilton

January 2025 · 10 min read

American spirit meets Swiss precision. Founded in Lancaster, Pennsylvania in 1892, Hamilton built its reputation timing America's railroads before becoming Hollywood's favorite watch brand.

Railroad Timekeeping

Hamilton's first calling was the American railroad. After a devastating train collision caused by inaccurate watches, railroads demanded precision timekeepers. Hamilton's railroad watches became the standard.

Hollywood's Choice

Hamilton has appeared in over 500 films—more than any other watch brand. The Ventura appeared on Elvis's wrist in "Blue Hawaii" (1961).

Key Collections

  • Khaki Field: Military heritage
  • Khaki Aviation: Pilot watches
  • Ventura: The iconic Elvis watch
  • Jazzmaster: Elegant dress watches

In 1974, Hamilton moved production to Switzerland. Today, all Hamilton watches are Swiss Made, combining American design heritage with Swiss manufacturing excellence.
