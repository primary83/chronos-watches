How to Choose

For Samsung Phone Users

Galaxy Watch 7 or Ultra is the obvious choice. Samsung-exclusive features like blood pressure monitoring and seamless integration make it the best match for your Galaxy phone.

For Pixel Phone Users

Pixel Watch 3 offers the cleanest integration with your Pixel phone. Google Assistant, call screening features, and seamless Fitbit health tracking make it worth the premium.

For Other Android Users

Any Wear OS watch works well. Samsung Galaxy Watch offers the most features. Garmin is best for athletes. Amazfit delivers the best value for budget-conscious buyers.

The Bottom Line

Android users have never had better smart watch options. Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 is the best overall choice for most people—excellent hardware, comprehensive health features, and full Google app support.

Athletes should consider Garmin for its unmatched training analytics. Budget-conscious buyers can get 80% of the experience for 50% of the price with Amazfit. And Pixel owners will love the seamless Google integration of Pixel Watch 3.

Unlike iPhone users who have exactly one good option, you have choices. That's the Android advantage.
