About Cartier

Founded in 1847 in Paris, Cartier created the first modern wristwatch (Santos, 1904) and the iconic Tank (1917). Part of Richemont Group. Known for elegant design, blue cabochon crowns, and Roman numeral dials. Read full Cartier history →
