Choosing a Women's Watch

Key considerations for women's timepieces:

  • Case size: 26-34mm traditional; 36-40mm for those preferring larger watches
  • Movement: Quartz for accuracy/thinness; automatic for mechanical appreciation
  • Versatility: Consider dress-to-casual flexibility
  • Bracelet vs strap: Metal for durability; leather for elegance
  • Complications: Date, moon phase, or time-only simplicity
