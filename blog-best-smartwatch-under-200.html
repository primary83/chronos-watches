Best Smart Watches Under $200 in 2026

You don't need to spend $400+ for a great smart watch. These budget-friendly picks deliver fitness tracking, notifications, and style without breaking the bank.

What to Expect Under $200

At this price point, you can get impressive features that were premium-only a few years ago:

• AMOLED displays (on better models)
• Built-in GPS for outdoor tracking
• Heart rate monitoring and SpO2
• 7-14 day battery life
• Water resistance for swimming
• Basic notification support

What you'll typically sacrifice: advanced health features (ECG, blood pressure), extensive app ecosystems, and premium build materials like titanium or sapphire glass.

What About Apple Watch SE?

The Apple Watch SE starts at $249—just above our budget. If you're an iPhone user and can stretch your budget slightly, it's worth considering. You get the full Apple ecosystem, excellent health features, and guaranteed software updates. But if $200 is your ceiling, the picks above offer better value.

How We Chose These Picks

We evaluated dozens of budget smart watches on:

• Display quality: AMOLED preferred for visibility and battery efficiency
• Battery life: Multi-day minimum; week+ preferred
• GPS accuracy: Built-in GPS for outdoor tracking without phone
• Health features: Heart rate, SpO2, sleep tracking basics
• Build quality: Water resistance, durable construction
• Value: Features relative to price point

The Bottom Line

The Amazfit GTR 4 offers the best overall package under $200—premium display, incredible battery, accurate GPS, and classic design. If fitness tracking is your priority, the Fitbit Charge 6 can't be beat. And if you want maximum savings, the Amazfit Bip 5 at $89 is remarkable value.
