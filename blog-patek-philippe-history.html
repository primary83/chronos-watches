The History of Patek Philippe

December 2024 · 12 min read

You never actually own a Patek Philippe. You merely look after it for the next generation. The most prestigious name in watchmaking since 1839.

Antoni Patek and Francois Czapek founded the company in 1839 in Geneva. Jean Adrien Philippe joined in 1845, bringing his revolutionary keyless winding system.

The Stern family has owned Patek Philippe since 1932, ensuring continuity and independence. Every watch receives the Patek Philippe Seal, guaranteeing lifetime service.

Key Collections

Nautilus, Aquanaut, Calatrava, Grand Complications, Twenty-4

Patek Philippe remains the ultimate status symbol in watchmaking, with vintage pieces regularly setting auction records.
