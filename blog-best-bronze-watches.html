Buying Guide

Best Bronze Watches 2025

10 min read Updated January 2025

Bronze watches have captured the hearts of collectors who appreciate living materials that develop unique character over time. The natural patina that forms on bronze creates a one-of-a-kind timepiece that tells the story of its wearer's adventures.

Why Bronze?

  • Unique patina — Each watch develops its own character based on wear and environment
  • Historical connection — Bronze has been used in maritime equipment for centuries
  • Warm aesthetics — Complements a wide range of strap colors
  • Durability — Highly resistant to saltwater corrosion
  • Hypoallergenic options — Many use nickel-free CuSn8 bronze

Top Bronze Watches

Tudor Black Bay Bronze

$4,275

Tudor's bronze diver is the benchmark for the category. The aluminum bronze alloy (CuAl) develops a warm, chocolatey patina while resisting the green verdigris some dislike. The brown dial complements the case beautifully.

Case: 43mm Bronze
Movement: MT5601
Power Reserve: 70 hours
Water Resistance: 200m

Oris Carl Brashear Calibre 401

$4,400

A tribute to the first African-American U.S. Navy Master Diver. Features Oris's 5-day power reserve movement in a beautifully finished bronze case. The blue dial and vintage-inspired design are stunning.

Case: 40mm Bronze
Movement: Oris 401
Power Reserve: 120 hours
Water Resistance: 100m

Bell & Ross BR 03-92 Diver Bronze

$5,100

Bell & Ross's distinctive square case in bronze creates a unique tool watch aesthetic. The green dial plays beautifully off the bronze case as it develops patina.

Case: 42mm Bronze
Movement: BR-CAL.302
Power Reserve: 54 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Panerai Bronzo Submersible PAM00968

$17,100

Panerai pioneered the modern bronze watch trend with their Bronzo series. The 47mm case offers impressive wrist presence, and Panerai's bronze develops beautiful blue-green patina.

Case: 47mm Bronze
Movement: P.9010
Power Reserve: 72 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Affordable Bronze Options

Seiko Prospex "Willard" Bronze SPB244

$1,400

Seiko brings bronze to their iconic Willard case design. Excellent value with Seiko's reliable 6R35 movement and 200m water resistance.

Case: 42.7mm Bronze
Movement: Seiko 6R35
Power Reserve: 70 hours
Water Resistance: 200m

Zelos Mako V3 Bronze

$399

Incredible value in a bronze diver. The microbrand Zelos has earned respect for quality bronze cases at accessible prices. Limited editions sell out quickly.

Case: 40mm Bronze
Movement: Miyota 9015
Power Reserve: 42 hours
Water Resistance: 300m

Caring for Bronze

Bronze watches require minimal maintenance. To slow patina, wipe with a dry cloth after wear. To accelerate patina naturally, wear it often! To remove patina, use lemon juice or a bronze polish. Many owners simply let nature take its course.

Our Recommendation

For most buyers, the Tudor Black Bay Bronze offers the best combination of quality, value, and controlled patina development. For those wanting the original bronze dive watch experience, the Panerai Bronzo started it all.
