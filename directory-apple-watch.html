Which Apple Watch Should You Buy?

Apple Watch SE: Best for first-time buyers, kids, or budget-conscious users who want core smartwatch features without premium extras.

Apple Watch Series 10: Best for most people. Full health features (ECG, blood oxygen), always-on display, latest processor. The sweet spot.

Apple Watch Series 10 Titanium: For those wanting premium materials and sapphire crystal durability. Cellular included.

Apple Watch Ultra 2: For serious athletes, outdoor adventurers, divers, or anyone wanting maximum battery life and durability. Overkill for casual users.

Apple Watch Hermès: For fashion-forward luxury buyers who want exclusive designs and premium leather craftsmanship.

Note: Apple Watch requires iPhone 8 or later with iOS 17+. Cellular models require compatible carrier plan.
